Multi-Stage Compressed Dual-Flow Wind Turbine Generator

Wind energy scales with the cube of wind speed. This system captures frontal airflow for centrifugal power generation and uses controlled exhaust to minimize drag. A single central turbine with symmetrically positioned side generators supports stable operation across mobility and infrastructure applications.

Applications

Passenger vehicles, buses, trucks, ships, rail systems, and aviation concepts.

Design Intent

Convert relative airflow into usable electricity while reducing resistance through controlled exhaust.

Integration

Pairs with storage and microgrid systems for charging, resilience, and off-grid operation.

Seawater Desalination Using a Gravity-Driven Natural Filtration Tower

XPRIZE Water Scarcity Competition – Track B Qualified Team. A gravity-driven, multi-layer filtration approach designed for field deployment in coastal and island regions.

Core Principle

Seawater is converted into freshwater by passing through engineered material layers that absorb salinity. The layers are regenerated through periodic washing for repeated use.
