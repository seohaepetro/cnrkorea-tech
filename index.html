Technologies

Core technologies summarized from the deck (minimum set, infrastructure-ready).

Multi-Stage Compressed Dual-Flow Wind Turbine Generator

Captures frontal airflow for centrifugal generation and controls exhaust to minimize drag. Single central turbine with symmetrically positioned side generators for stable, tip-free operation.

All-Purpose Turbine Generator

Applicable to wind, in-stream hydropower, tidal energy, and thermal steam systems—enabling simultaneous power generation, seawater desalination, waste-heat utilization, and wastewater treatment with water reuse.

Complete Combustion Incinerator (CCI)

Processes all waste types without separation at temperatures above 2,500°C. After combustion, only inorganic residues (iron, glass, stone) remain for recycling, while generating electricity and desalinated freshwater from seawater.

Future-Oriented Carbon Emission Reduction System

Low and wide stack design to enhance particulate matter removal. After fine dust removal, CO₂ is captured and reused by diluting with water (carbonated water), depending on application needs (e.g., agricultural water management).

Soil & Marine Pollution Remediation

Remediation of large-scale soil and marine contamination using equipment-operated hot-water circulation systems without direct human involvement.

Non-Entry Crude Oil Storage Tank Cleaning

Automated, non-entry tank cleaning using a hot-water recycling system—eliminating human access to confined spaces and avoiding robotic equipment.

Gravity-Driven Natural Filtration Tower Desalination

Seawater is converted into freshwater by passing through an engineered material layer that absorbs salinity. The layer is regenerated through periodic washing for repeated use. (XPRIZE Water Scarcity Competition – Track B Qualified Team)

Water Recycling & Reuse System

Integrated wastewater treatment (WWTP) with reuse pathways: potable supply, industrial use, and make-up water sourced from rivers, streams, and rainwater.

Programs

New City / Industrial Complex Program

Post-war and post-disaster reconstruction and future-oriented new city development: modular infrastructure combining waste management, power, desalination, and water reuse.

River & Coastal Infrastructure Program

Deployable across rivers and coastal regions (e.g., large river systems) to integrate in-stream/tidal power with wastewater treatment and water reuse, improving regional resilience.
