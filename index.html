System Flow

Integrated workflow that converts waste into useful energy and water, enabling desalination, wastewater purification, and practical reuse—while reducing emissions.

Integrated Workflow Overview

Waste → Complete combustion → Heat recovery → Turbine power → Electricity supply → Desalination → Wastewater treatment → Water reuse → Emission reduction → Circular system.

Key outcomes
  • Reduces landfill reliance by converting mixed waste into useful energy.
  • Reuses recovered heat for desalination and industrial water production.
  • Enables wastewater purification and treated-water reuse at scale.
  • Integrates emission reduction to support low-carbon infrastructure.
  • Designed for modular deployment (cities, islands, industrial zones, reconstruction).
