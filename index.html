Integrated Water–Energy–Environment Platform

Recovering Heat. Reusing Water. Generating Power.

CNR Korea develops integrated environmental infrastructure that converts waste heat into usable energy, enables seawater desalination, and reuses treated wastewater—supporting resilient cities, industrial zones, islands, and coastal regions.
